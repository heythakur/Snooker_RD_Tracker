RD Rules
Setup: 6 colours on usual spots. 1 Red (Cherry) between Pink and Black.

Points: Y=2, G=3, Br=4, Bl=5, P=6, Bk=7, Cherry=10.

Gameplay: Call shot (ball and pocket). Hit called ball first, pot in called pocket. Safe shot allowed (hit called ball, don't pot). NO SAFE on Cherry.

Cherry Rules:
- +10 if potted, -10 if missed.
- Cannot be attempted (pot) if on absolute 0 score initially. Once a player moves from 0, it is enabled for the rest of the game.
- Must meet Compulsory Pots and Attempts to exit. Extra pots pay off attempts.
- A miss or auto-foul does not count towards attempts if it breaks 3-in-a-row. Accidental hits do not deduct attempts.

3-in-a-Row Rule: Max 3 consecutive pots of same colour in a break (including Cherry). 4th is auto-foul (minimum 4 points or ball value) and ends the break.

Break Rules: Must hit Pink or Black off break from D. In-hand but from D after scratch (any ball can be hit).

Exit Rules: Reach target, meet cherries, end turn legally (no foul). Target Score Cap: If crossing target but fouling, score caps at target, then deduct foul.
